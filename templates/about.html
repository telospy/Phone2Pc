About Phone2PC

Instant file transfer between any devices

📱 What is Phone2PC?

Phone2PC is a free, open-source file transfer tool that lets you share files between any devices - Phone to PC, PC to Phone, and PC to PC - instantly with a QR code.

⚡ How It Works

1

Create Your Space

Get your unique random link

2

Start Server on PC

Enter your ngrok token and select where files are saved

3

Scan QR Code with Phone

Your phone connects instantly to your PC

4

Transfer Files!

Upload from phone to PC, download from PC to phone

🔄 Transfer Modes

📱➡️💻

Phone to PC

Upload photos, videos, documents from your phone directly to your computer

💻➡️📱

PC to Phone

Download files from your PC to your phone with one tap

💻➡️💻

PC to PC

Share files between computers without USB drives or cables

🔒 Security & Privacy

  • ✅ End-to-end encrypted transfers via ngrok
  • ✅ Links expire after 2 hours automatically
  • ✅ Files stay on your PC - never stored on our servers
  • ✅ Secret links prevent unauthorized access
  • ✅ 100% open source - inspect the code yourself
  • 📦 500MB per file limit (ngrok free tier limitation)
  • ⏱️ 2-hour session limit (ngrok free tier limitation)

⚠️ Ngrok Free Tier Limitations:

  • • 500MB maximum file size per transfer
  • • 2-hour session time limit
  • • 40 connections per minute
